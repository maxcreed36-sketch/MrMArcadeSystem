Score QR Generator

Generate a QR code for scores. For negative scores, it includes only the minus score and current date/time (no username). For positive scores, it includes username (if provided), plus score, and current date/time.

Format: /Username?/+/-Score?/Date Time
- Negative score example: /-123/2023-10-05 14:30:00
- Positive score example: /user123/+456/2023-10-05 14:30:00
