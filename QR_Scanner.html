QR Code Scanner & Data Generator

Scan a QR code to capture username, then add a score to generate formatted data with current date and time.

1. Scan QR Code
Camera not started. Click "Start Camera" to begin.
2. User Information
3. Generate Output
Output
/Username?/+Score?/DateAndTime

How to use:

  1. Click "Start Camera" and allow camera access
  2. Point your camera at a QR code to scan it
  3. The username will be automatically captured
  4. Enter or modify the score value
  5. Click "Generate Formatted Data" to create the output
